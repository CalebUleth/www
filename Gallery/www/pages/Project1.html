Project 1

Our first project was to construct a reaction to the documentery "The Light Bulb Conspiracy" using the priciples of photo realisim. My interpetation was quite literal, a member of the mafia silloetting a road covered in broken lightbulbs.

To construct this image I started with a image of a street with a lamp.

Not wanting to photoshop in the hundreds of lightbulbs I would need, I opted to make a digital twin of the scene and add the lightbulbs to that via physics.

And then composited them together. To get the silhouette I started with this image

Modified the contrast, duplicated it several times, then used layer blends to turn it into a black and white image, which I then removed the white from and overlayed onto the previous composit.
